Burger Recipe



Ingredients

  • 1 Burger Bun
  • 1 Beef Patty
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cheese
  • Onion
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

Instructions

  1. Grill the beef patty until cooked to your liking.
  2. Toast the burger bun.
  3. Assemble the burger with lettuce, tomato, cheese, onion, ketchup, and mustard.
  4. Serve and enjoy!
