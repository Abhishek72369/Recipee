Momos Recipe



Ingredients

  • 1 cup All-purpose Flour
  • 1/2 cup Water
  • 1 cup Minced Chicken or Vegetables
  • 1 Onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 1 tsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Mix flour and water to make a dough. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. In a bowl, mix minced chicken or vegetables with onion, soy sauce, ginger-garlic paste, salt, and pepper.
  3. Roll out the dough and cut into small circles.
  4. Place a spoonful of filling in the center of each circle and fold to seal.
  5. Steam the momos for 10-15 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with dipping sauce.
