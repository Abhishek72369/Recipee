Pizza Recipe



Ingredients

  • 1 Pizza Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Bell Peppers
  • Olives

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a baking sheet.
  3. Spread tomato sauce over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top.
  5. Add pepperoni, bell peppers, and olives.
  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until the crust is golden brown.
  7. Slice and serve!
